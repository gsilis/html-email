This email is meantto showcase how emails can be designed for different mediums. Taking into account things like lack of image support, lack of support of certain style capabilities, and device dimensions.
On devices or machines that are can display emails wide enough, you can show content side by side - taking advantage of the horizontal space that exists. On narrower screens, the content can be stacked. Using a media query, you can force the stacked content to fill the available width.
For clients that support negative margins, you can have graphics overlap their containers.
But make sure the design still looks good if the graphic cannot be pushed up via CSS.
If wide image-based text objects must be used, make sure you design them for non-squeezed layout @600px and mobile layout @~300px. Also, make sure to try out all standard fonts to ensure they still give the desired look and feel.
